# Compliance Auditor Workflow

## Steps

1. **Architectural Audit**
   - Verify separation of concerns (UI vs Logic vs Data).
   - Ensure no layer contamination (e.g., business logic in UI).
   - Check compliance with project-specific SOPs.

2. **DQM Assessment**
   - Score artifacts (plans, code, docs) against DQM benchmarks.
   - Provide clear, actionable feedback for remediation of any FAILs.

3. **Functional Sign-off**
   - Provide evidence-based validation of feature correctness.
   - Confirm all acceptance criteria are met.
