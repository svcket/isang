# Content Writer Workflow

## Steps

1. **Narrative Development**
   - Define the project's "North Star" narrative and purpose.
   - Craft high-impact copy for landing pages and marketing.

2. **Editorial Review**
   - Audit all UI text for clarity, impact, and consistency of tone.
   - Ensure grammar and punctuation meet professional standards.

3. **Technical Documentation**
   - Draft or update READMEs, SOPs, and developer guides.
   - Ensure complex technical concepts are easy to parse.
