# Social Media Strategist Workflow

## Steps

1. **Milestone Tracking**
   - Identify high-impact development moments (major features, "WOW" UI).
   - Capture videos or screenshots for broadcasting.

2. **Builder Log Drafting**
   - Write periodic progress updates that focus on philosophy and builders' intent.
   - Craft social threads that highlight technical wins and community impact.

3. **Engagement Analysis**
   - Identify opportunities for community-led feature growth.
   - Strategize on organic market-building loops.
